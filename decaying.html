Decaying Winter Scaling

"Decaying Winter" offers a unique take on difficulty scaling, distinguishing itself from conventional games by prioritizing team dynamics and strategic cooperation.

Unlike traditional games where difficulty often relies solely on individual skill levels or preset challenges, this game implements a punishing debuff system.

Each player's survival becomes crucial, as the death of any team member triggers a morale debuff for all remaining players up to 4 times.

Why IS it good??

Each morale debuff incurred due to the loss of a team member initiates a terrible consequence, systematically lowering all individual stats of the inflicted player.

Players must navigate the dual challenge of maintaining team morale while safeguarding their own survival.

With each debuff, players face diminishing abilities and heightened vulnerability, amplifying the tension and strategic depth of the gameplay.

This is incredibly great for a sense of reward when the morale debuff wears off after a few nights, giving you relief and extra strength for the next wave. However there is something, GREATLY terrible approaching.
